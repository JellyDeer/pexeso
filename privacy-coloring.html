Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how information related to the use of mobile applications and games published under the JellyDeer brand is processed. This document has been prepared in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR) and other applicable laws.

Our goal is to minimize the amount of processed data and protect the privacy of all users, especially children.

2. Data Controller

The data controller is the operator of the JellyDeer application. Questions regarding privacy or data protection rights may be sent to the email address listed below.

3. Information We Process

The application does not require an account and does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information.

Technical information required for Google Play distribution, purchase verification and app functionality may be processed.

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • Device language
  • Technical identifiers provided by Google Play
  • Purchased digital content information

4. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Full name
  • Postal address
  • Phone number
  • User email address
  • Device location
  • Biometric data
  • Photos, videos or contacts

5. Legal Basis under GDPR

Where applicable, data is processed under Article 6(1)(b), Article 6(1)(c) and Article 6(1)(f) of the GDPR.

6. Locally Stored Data

The application stores game progress, unlocked content, settings and other operational information locally on the user's device. This data normally never leaves the device.

Deleting the application or clearing its storage may permanently remove this data.

7. Google Play Services

The application is distributed through Google Play. Google may process technical information required for licensing, updates, security and distribution according to its own privacy policies.

8. Google Play Billing

The application may offer optional one-time purchases. All payments are securely processed by Google through Google Play Billing. The application operator never receives payment card details.

9. Advertising and Analytics

The application currently does not use third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, Firebase Analytics or similar tracking technologies.

10. Cookies and Device Identifiers

Mobile applications generally do not use cookies like websites. However, the operating system or platform may use technical identifiers required for purchases and security.

11. Children's Privacy (GDPR-K & COPPA)

This application is intended for children and families. We do not knowingly collect children's personal information and do not provide user accounts, chat features or public profiles.

12. International Data Transfers

Some Google services may process data outside the European Economic Area under Google's legal safeguards.

13. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary. Local application data remains under the user's control.
